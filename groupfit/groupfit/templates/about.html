About GroupFit

GroupFit is a dynamic fitness website that makes it easier than ever to work out with your friends. Any group (group of friends, yoga class, college wrestling team, etc) can add motivation and fun to exercise with exciting features like playlists and color-coded graphs. So invite your friends, set your goal, and watch your group make progress with each workout to ultimate fitness!
